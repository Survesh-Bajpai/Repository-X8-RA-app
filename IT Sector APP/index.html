Research Methodology

üìä DCF Valuation Methodology

Free Cash Flow Projection

  • Revenue Growth: Based on historical trends, management guidance, and industry growth rates
  • Operating Margins: Analyzed considering automation benefits, wage inflation, and pricing pressure
  • Tax Rate: Effective tax rate considering global operations and tax optimization strategies
  • CAPEX: Asset-light model with focus on technology infrastructure investments

Terminal Value Calculation

  • Terminal Growth Rate: 3.0% to 4.0% based on long-term GDP growth expectations
  • Exit Multiple: Cross-verified with EV/EBITDA multiples of mature IT companies

Discount Rate (WACC)

  • Risk-free Rate: 10-year G-Sec yield (7.1%)
  • Equity Risk Premium: 6.5% for Indian markets
  • Beta: Company-specific beta based on 2-year daily returns
  • Cost of Debt: Based on current borrowing rates and credit profile

üìà Relative Valuation Framework

Peer Group Selection

  • NIFTY 500 IT universe for comprehensive coverage
  • Business model similarity and revenue size considerations
  • Geographic exposure and client concentration analysis

Valuation Multiples

  • P/E Ratio: Forward PE based on consensus earnings estimates
  • EV/EBITDA: Enterprise value to EBITDA multiple
  • Price/Book: For asset-intensive business segments
  • EV/Sales: For growth companies with limited profitability
